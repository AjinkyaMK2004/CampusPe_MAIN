FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an Ad?

Click on Post an Ad button on the top right corner. It will redirect you to the Post An Ad page.


Enter the details as prompted in the form. Enter your correct details in the forms as the buyers would contact you using those details. After posting an Ad, wait for the admin to approve the posts. If you want your ad to be featured, please contact us.

Who should I to contact if I have any question?

Please visit the contact details page. You will find the official email id there.


How do I purchase the product listed?

We are just the facilitator between the buyer and the seller. You have to contact the respected seller in order to purchase the product.


How do I contact the seller?

You can contact the individual via his email address or phone number listed in the ad. It is suggested that you initiate contact with the individual via WhatsApp before calling directly.
